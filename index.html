Welcome to Gambit Chess ("the App", "we", "our", or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use Gambit Chess. By using Gambit Chess, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

§ 01

Information We Collect

Depending on the features you use, we may collect:

Account Information

  • Username
  • Email address (if you create an account)
  • Profile picture (if uploaded)

Game Data

  • Match history
  • Wins, losses, draws
  • Ratings (ELO)
  • Level progression
  • Achievements
  • Game settings
  • Saved games
  • Chess puzzles progress

Multiplayer Information

  • Player IDs
  • Matchmaking information
  • Friends list (if implemented)
  • Game invitations
  • Online status during gameplay

Device Information

  • Device model
  • Operating system version
  • App version
  • Language settings
  • Crash logs
  • Performance diagnostics

Optional Information

If you choose to provide it, we may also collect:

  • Profile avatar
  • Display name
  • Feedback submitted through the app
§ 02

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide gameplay features.
  • Save your progress.
  • Synchronize your account across devices.
  • Enable multiplayer chess matches.
  • Improve game performance.
  • Detect bugs and crashes.
  • Prevent cheating and abuse.
  • Respond to support requests.
  • Improve future versions of Gambit Chess.
§ 03

Local Storage

Some information may be stored locally on your device, including:

  • Game progress
  • Settings
  • Saved games
  • Preferences
  • Offline data

This information remains on your device unless removed by you or when uninstalling the application.

§ 04

Cloud Storage

If cloud synchronization is enabled, your game data may be securely stored on our servers to allow progress synchronization across multiple devices.

§ 05

Multiplayer Services

When using online multiplayer:

  • Your username may be visible to other players.
  • Match history may be stored.
  • Ratings and leaderboard information may be publicly displayed.

We do not intentionally expose your email address or other private account information to other players.

§ 06

Analytics

We may collect anonymous analytics and crash reports to:

  • Improve stability
  • Fix bugs
  • Enhance gameplay
  • Monitor performance

Analytics data does not intentionally identify you personally.

§ 07

Advertising

If advertisements are displayed within Gambit Chess, third-party advertising providers may collect limited device information as permitted by their own privacy policies.

If the app does not contain advertisements, no advertising-related data is collected.

§ 08

Purchases

If premium features or in-app purchases are offered, payments are processed securely by the respective app store or payment provider.

We do not collect or store your payment card information.

§ 09

Data Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards to help protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

However, no method of electronic storage or transmission over the internet is completely secure.

§ 10

Children's Privacy

Gambit Chess is not intended for children under the age required by applicable law in their jurisdiction without parental consent. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children in violation of applicable laws.

§ 11

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:

  • Access your personal information.
  • Correct inaccurate information.
  • Delete your account and associated data.
  • Request a copy of your stored data.
  • Withdraw consent where applicable.

To exercise these rights, please contact us.

§ 12

Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to provide the services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

§ 13

Third-Party Services

Gambit Chess may use trusted third-party services such as:

  • Authentication providers
  • Cloud hosting providers
  • Multiplayer infrastructure
  • Crash reporting services
  • Analytics providers

These services process information in accordance with their own privacy policies.

§ 14

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updated versions will be posted within the application or on the official website.

Continued use of Gambit Chess after changes become effective constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

§ 15

Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the official support channel provided within Gambit Chess or on our official website.
